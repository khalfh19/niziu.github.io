About Us

A global girl group selected from 10,000 people from the joint audition project "Nizi Project" of Sony Music x JYP

The pre-debut digital mini album "Make you happy" achieved the feat of winning the first place in the digital ranking of "the first female artist" in three categories at the same time, and the digital album exceeded 100,000 downloads. Achieved the crown.

The cumulative number of views of the lead track "Make you happy" has exceeded 200 million, and the number of YouTube views of Music Video

In December 2020, he made his long-desired debut with the single "Step and a step", and shined at No. 1 on the Oricon weekly single ranking and Billboard JAPAN's general chart.

At the end of 2020, he won the special prize of the "62nd Japan Record Awards" and participated in the "71st NHK Kouhaku Uta Gassen" for the first time in a short period of "only 29 days after his debut", giving him an overwhelming presence. Burned into the viewer.

In April 2021, he released his 2nd single "Take a picture / Poppin'Shakin'", which won the 1st place on the Oricon weekly single ranking and Billboard JAPAN's general chart, and is currently 123 on the worldwide music distribution chart. Achieved the crown and is still recording a smash hit.

And on July 5th (Monday), NiziU 's first summer song, and "Super Summer", which has been decided to be used as a Coca-Cola CM song, will be released for distribution.
